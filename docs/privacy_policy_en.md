## 1. Who We Are

This Privacy Policy describes how **`[Operator]`** (“we”, “us”) handles information when you use the mobile application **Twingl Road** (the “App”).

## 2. Information We Process

### 2.1 Location information

If you grant permission, the App may access **precise location** from your device (e.g., latitude/longitude) to detect areas you pass through and to provide audio guidance. Depending on platform settings, **background location** may be used while guiding is active.

You can withdraw location permission in your **device settings**. Without it, location-based features may not work.

### 2.2 Information stored on your device

The App may store the following **locally on your device** (for example using platform storage APIs):

- **Guide/route history**, such as city names, timestamps, portions of generated narration, and related link metadata  
- **Preferences**, such as narrative style, custom persona titles/descriptions, and selected text-to-speech (TTS) voice identifiers  

This draft assumes such data is **not automatically uploaded to our servers**. If we introduce cloud backup or sync, we will update this Policy and notify you as required by law.

### 2.3 AI narration and voice output

To generate narration, the App may send **context such as city/region names and your persona settings** into **on-device AI or OS-provided language features** (for example Apple on-device models where available). How those systems process data is also governed by **your platform provider’s policies**.

**Text-to-speech** may use OS or vendor voices you select.

### 2.4 Information you provide

When you create **custom personas**, your text is stored on your device and used to shape narration. Do not submit sensitive personal data or content that infringes others’ rights.

### 2.5 Links and third-party services

When you open **maps, search, or websites** from the App, those services collect information under **their own privacy policies**. We do not control them.

### 2.6 Analytics

This draft assumes we **do not** integrate advertising or behavioral analytics SDKs. If we add them, we will disclose what is collected and seek consent where required.
