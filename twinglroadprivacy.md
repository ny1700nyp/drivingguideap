## 1. Scope

This Privacy Policy describes how information is handled when you use the mobile application **Twingl Road** (the “App”).

## 2. Information we process

### 2.1 Location

If you grant permission, the App accesses **device location** (e.g. latitude and longitude) to detect areas you pass through and to drive location-based narration and labels. Depending on your choices and platform APIs, **background location** may be used while location guiding is active.

You can withdraw permission in **system settings**. Without location access, core location-based features may not work.

### 2.2 Data stored on your device

The App may store **locally** (for example via platform storage APIs):

- **Guide / route history** — such as place labels used for a session, timestamps, portions of generated narration, and related link metadata  
- **Preferences** — such as narrative style, custom persona titles and descriptions, and selected text-to-speech (TTS) voice identifiers  

This Policy assumes such data is **not automatically uploaded to our servers**. If we introduce cloud backup, accounts, or sync, we will update this Policy and notify you as required by applicable law.

### 2.3 Reverse geocoding and offline place names

To turn coordinates into readable place names, the App may use **platform reverse geocoding** when network conditions allow. When that does not yield a name, the App may use **bundled offline geographic data** on the device to suggest a **nearest city** and approximate distance. That processing stays **on device**.

### 2.4 AI narration and voice

To produce narration, the App may pass **context such as city or region names and your persona settings** into **on-device AI or OS-provided language features** where the platform supports them. Processing by Apple, Google, or other platform vendors is also governed by **their policies** and your device settings.

**Text-to-speech** uses OS or vendor voices you select.

When on-device generation is **not** used, the App may use **template-based narration** so you still receive spoken content without sending prompts to our own backend servers.

### 2.5 Weather (fallback path only)

When template-based fallback narration runs, the App **may** request **short current weather text** from **Open‑Meteo** (`api.open-meteo.com`) using **latitude and longitude** (rounded for the request) and **no account or API key** in the App. That service operates under **Open‑Meteo’s own terms and privacy practices**. If you prefer not to rely on that path, use configurations or OS versions where on-device generation is available, where applicable.

### 2.6 Information you provide

**Custom personas** store your text **on your device** and influence prompts. Do not submit sensitive personal data or third‑party content you are not allowed to use.

### 2.7 Links and third-party services

Opening **maps, search, or websites** from the App subjects you to those providers’ **own privacy policies**. We do not control them.

### 2.8 Analytics

This Policy assumes we **do not** integrate advertising or behavioral analytics SDKs in the App as shipped. If that changes, we will disclose it and obtain consent where required.

## 3. Legal bases (where applicable)

## 5. International transfers

This Policy assumes **no routine transfer** of your App data to our own servers abroad. If that changes, we will describe safeguards as applicable (e.g. Standard Contractual Clauses).

## 6. Retention

- In-app actions may delete specific records (e.g. clearing history).  
- Records we must keep for legal compliance are retained only as long as required.

## 7. Security
