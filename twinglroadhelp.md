Help and contact information for the **Twingl Road** mobile app. You can publish this file as a GitHub Gist, host it on your site, or link it from App Store Connect as your **Support URL** once it is reachable over HTTPS.

## Contact

**Support email:** [mytwingl@twinglapp.com](mailto:mytwingl@twinglapp.com)

When reporting an issue, please include:

- Device model  
- OS version (e.g. iOS 18.x)  
- What you were doing when it happened  
- Screenshots or screen recordings if helpful  

We aim to respond within a few business days.

## Before you email

### Location and narration

The app uses your location to tailor narration to the area you are passing through. Grant **Location** permission while using the app. If you use guiding while driving, **background location** may be required depending on your settings.

Weak GPS, parking structures, or tunnels can delay or skip updates until the signal improves.

### Voice (text-to-speech)

Spoken guidance depends on **system text-to-speech** and your **language / voice** settings. If audio is missing or uses an unexpected voice, check **Settings → Accessibility** (Spoken Content and related options) and system language settings.

### On-device features

Some narration paths rely on **OS version and device capability**. On hardware or OS builds where a capability is unavailable, the app may use an alternative path. That behavior can be normal.

### Safety

Use Twingl Road only in compliance with **local traffic laws**. Prefer **hands-free** use; passengers can interact with the app when it is safe to do so. Do not use the app in any way that distracts you while operating a vehicle.
